About Us

Pura vida spa is today the only spa facility in Corcovado, Costa Rica. We offer a genuine experience in a harmonious environment, in the middle of Costa Rica's rain forest. In our spa you will find pools both indoors and outdoors, a relaxation area, exclusive treatments with certified specialists, a restaurant with a beautiful view and 6 unique hotel rooms.
