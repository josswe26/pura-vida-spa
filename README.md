# Pura Vida Spa

![Pura Vida Spa mockup images]()

Pura Vida Spa is a fictional spa located in the heart of the Costa Rican rainforest. Pura Vida Spa offers a variety of beauty treatments and its location allows you to enjoy their services while surrounded by the beautiful Costa Rican nature.

The purpose of this website is to promote Pura Vida Spa and to provide information about the spa, its treatments and contact options. The website targets an audience of new customers looking for the treatments being offered as well as recurrent users looking to schedule a new appointments or find out about their new offerings.

Visit the deployed website [here](https://josswe26.github.io/pura-vida-spa/)
